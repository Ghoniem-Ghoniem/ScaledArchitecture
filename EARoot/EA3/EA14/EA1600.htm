Requirement Type Status Difficulty Priority Stability
Autonomous reports Extracting and Submitting Functional Proposed Medium Medium Moderate
Details:
Extracting reports and Submitting them to GRP System will be fully autonomous
GRP01: Manual triggering of calculation Functional Proposed Medium Medium Moderate
Details:
Finanacial officer should trigger the process of calculating the monthly invoices, while recalculations should be triggered by L2 Support agent
GRP02 : Separation of processing Functional Proposed Medium Medium Moderate
Details:
Each invoice should be reviewed, approved, integrated with GRP independently from other invoices
GRP03 : Following calculation templates Functional Proposed Medium Medium Moderate
Details:
Calculate the invoices according to provided templates
GRP04: Downloadable invoices Functional Proposed Medium Medium Moderate
Details:
Download invoices in PDF and Excel formats
GRP05 : Systemized suppot communication Functional Proposed Medium Medium Moderate
Details:
Invalid invoices should be escalated to support through the system
GRP06 : DevOps adoption Functional Proposed Medium Medium Moderate
Details:
All invoice incidents should registered on DevOps before being resolved so they would follow incident management process

3. Push data to GRP System
